IT Service Management (ITSM) for the Modern Era

With a rapidly evolving way many businesses change how they interact with their customers, it is also important to have an I.T. plan that is as up-to-date as the times we are in.

Since 2011, YMR Technologies has been providing support and developing solutions for businesses of all sizes. Whether you are a cloud-based, or a small or medium sized business with a singular, multiple, or no locations at all, we are up to the challenge!

As a Managed Service Provider (MSP), our DevOps/ITIL service offer and commitment is simple: I.T. infrastructure, with email, phone, or even direct-messaging based support for you and your staff for issues related to computers, networking, servers, software support, and cloud services, including VoIP and cloud based phone systems.

Cloud-first Services

Our services are either delivered cloud-based, or we can help you transition your legacy systems to a cloud-first architecture based on Azure and/or AWS

Azure AD & O365

We are proud to partner with and offer Microsoft Azure AD & O365 implementation and management for your business

Networking & Security

Whether it is your on-premise networking, backup management, or monitoring your cloud-based systems, we can deliver and stay on top of it all!

User & License Management

Staying on top of all your system users and their licenses can be a complex task. We make it easy by taking care of it for you.

Software Integration & Support

Different suites of software are required in order to manage and operate your businesses, be it internal, or customer facing and we provide both solutions and support for this

Asset management & Break/Fix

It wouldn’t be I.T. without computers and peripherals, and you need reliable and quick support to make sure you and your employees are not affected by technological issues

Why Choose Us?

Modern businesses need I.T Consultants & and cannot always justify paying a CTO. That is when we can also come in and provide you with the capacity for us to act as a part-time CTO for your business!

The process and pricing is simple: For most scenarios, we charge a flat monthly rate depending on your infrastructure & users, and it starts with an environment assessment, which will allow us to provide you with the adequate solution for your needs.

Following the assessment, implementation of our monitoring and support tools, and forming management or team leads in order to make sure they know the processes to get the most out of your plan.
